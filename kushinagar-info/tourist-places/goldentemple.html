More About Mahaparinirvan Temple

Brief Information

The Golden temple is located near Kushalnagar, in Mysore-Madikeri road. This is a worship place of the Tibetans settled in kushalnagar and I cannot review a worship place. This is a beautifully built and neatly maintained temple where everybody irrespective of religion are allowed. Minimum half an hour is needed just to have a quick look at the temple, and paintings. This was a different experience in coorg, and I never expected to see something like this here. Visit there once to feel the beauty of Tibetan temple and the calmness. There is a Tibetan market near by, and enough parking space available.
